# InvoiceMY
This is a browser-based invoicing and ledger system for Malaysian freelancers and small businesses, supporting creation of invoices and credit notes

**📄InvoiceMY**

Many Malaysian freelancers and small business owners still create invoices manually using Word, Excel, or expensive accounting software. Managing invoices, issuing credit notes, tracking payments, and maintaining proper business records can quickly become time-consuming.

InvoiceMY is a lightweight browser-based invoicing and ledger management tool designed specifically for freelancers and small businesses in Malaysia.

The application allows users to generate professional invoices and credit notes, maintain multiple company profiles, track invoice statuses, and keep a complete transaction ledger — all without installing any software or creating an account.

**Features**

✅ No installation or registration required

✅ Generate professional PDF invoices

✅ Create and manage credit notes

✅ Multi-company support

✅ Invoice payment status tracking

✅ Built-in business ledger management

✅ WhatsApp payment reminder generation

✅ Local storage support for offline usage

✅ Support for SST information and Malaysian business details

✅ Browser-based and privacy-friendly

Perfect for freelancers, consultants, sole proprietors, and small businesses that need a simple yet powerful invoicing solution.
